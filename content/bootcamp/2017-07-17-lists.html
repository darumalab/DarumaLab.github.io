　リストの成分へアクセス

MyList2$first
## [1] 1 2 3
MyList2$second
## [1] "dog" "cat" NA
MyList2[[1]]
## [1] 1 2 3
MyList2[[2]]
## [1] "dog" "cat" NA
MyList2[["first"]]
## [1] 1 2 3
MyList2[["second"]]
## [1] "dog" "cat" NA
MyList2$first[2]
## [1] 2
MyList2[[2]][2:3]
## [1] "cat" NA
MyList2[["second"]][c(1, 2)]
## [1] "dog" "cat"
MyList2$first[2] <- 99

リストに新しい成分を加える

MyList2[[5]] <- 1:10

リストの成分削除

MyList2[[5]] <- NULL
